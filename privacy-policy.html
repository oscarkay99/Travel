3. Why We Collect It

  • Work Abroad applications: To assess eligibility and contact you about EU job placement opportunities. This is the primary purpose of the application form.
  • Trip enquiries: To follow up with a personalised travel proposal matching your stated destination and preferences.
  • WhatsApp communication: When you submit a form, a summary is also sent to our business WhatsApp number so our team can respond quickly.
  • Service improvement: Aggregated, anonymised data (e.g., which countries are most requested) may be reviewed to improve our services. This does not involve individual profiling.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. How Data Is Stored and Transmitted

When you submit a form:

  1. Your data is sent over HTTPS (TLS encryption) to our secure API hosted on a private VPS server.
  2. The API forwards your information by email to our team inbox using the Resend transactional email service.
  3. A copy of the key details is also delivered via WhatsApp to our business number (+233 55 949 9248) so we can respond promptly.
  4. Application data may be stored in a password-protected PostgreSQL database on the same VPS. Only authorised Rogernort staff have database access.

Our server is hosted on Hostinger VPS infrastructure with Ubuntu 24.04 LTS. All connections to the site and API use HTTPS certificates from Let's Encrypt.

5. Who Can Access Your Data

  • Rogernort staff — the owner and any staff members directly involved in processing your application or travel enquiry.
  • Resend — our transactional email provider, which processes and delivers emails on our behalf. Resend is subject to its own privacy policy and processes data as a data processor.
  • WhatsApp / Meta — when your summary is delivered to our WhatsApp, it passes through Meta's platform. Meta's privacy policy governs their handling of message content.
  • Hosting infrastructure — Hostinger provides the server on which data may be temporarily or persistently stored.

No other third parties receive your personal data.

6. How Long We Keep Data

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, and for a reasonable period afterwards in case of follow-up queries. In practice:

  • Active enquiries: Data is kept for the duration of the application or travel planning process.
  • Completed or declined enquiries: Retained for up to 24 months from the last contact, then deleted.
  • Email inbox copies: Subject to our email provider's standard retention. We review and purge old enquiries annually.

You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Your Rights).

7. Cookies and Local Storage

This website does not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party analytics scripts (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel).

We use localStorage — a browser-based storage mechanism — for two purposes:

  • Remembering your cookie consent preference so we do not show the banner on every visit.
  • Minor UI state (e.g., preloader dismissed).

No personal data is stored in cookies or localStorage. You can clear this data at any time from your browser settings under "Site Data" or "Storage".

External resources loaded by the site include Google Fonts (for typography) and Mixkit (for the hero video). These third-party domains may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies. Loading fonts requires your browser to make a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's standard logging practices.

8. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request that we erase your personal data. We will action this within 30 days unless we have a legal reason to retain it.
  • Objection — object to us processing your data for direct follow-up if you change your mind after submitting a form.
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
To exercise any of these rights, email us at akwaabatoursa@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Request" and the details of your request. We will respond within 14 business days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at persons under the age of 18. Our Work Abroad application form includes an age field with a minimum of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
